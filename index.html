NeuroLAMA

Welcome to NeuroLAMA ! The Neuroscience Longitudinal and Multimodal Analysis System

  • An open resource: all software is available free to investigators
  • Extensible: we will add machine-learning of progressively increasing complexity (Deep Learning algorithms such as RNNs and Transformers
  • The resource does not provide data, rather the analysis software is compatible with prominent Neuroscience research datasets (such as ADNI)
  • Our focus is on Alzheimer's and related Cognitive Decline conditions
