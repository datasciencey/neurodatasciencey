Data Sciencey

experiential data science

the experience of developing a solution,
that addresses a real need for a real consumer with real data &
your knowledge of the fundamentals in math, stats, and machine learning concepts &
your dexterity in applying data processing and machine learning tools &
your appreciation of the customer need and from the perspective of their domain &
your persistent mindfulness of that need from solution start-to-handover

DataSciencey is intended as a resource for anyone interested in hands-on data science, via projects. The projects are drawn from my (research) industry work in data science, and come with real data.
